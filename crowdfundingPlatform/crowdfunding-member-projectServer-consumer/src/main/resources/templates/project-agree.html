1 Agreement body

This agreement is initiated by the owners of the Andong website (including but not limited to www.atcrowd.com and AndongCrowdfunding platform) and their affiliates ( hereinafter referred to as " Andong ") on the AndongCrowdfunding platform and projects that log in and use the AndongCrowdfunding platform Signed by people (hereinafter referred to as "promoters")

2 Definitions

2.1 AndongCrowdfunding platform: The AndongCrowdfunding platform is a dream platform that can make the sponsor’s project plan a reality. It is a platform " dedicated to creating in China and realizing the dream of innovative entrepreneurs", and sponsors can initiate innovation on the AndongCrowdfunding platform The fundraising needs of the project, and promise to provide different forms of return to the supporters of the project, the supporters can express their support to the promoters by pre-purchasing the project results or related products of the promoters.

2.2 Sponsor: Refers to the legal person that initiated the project on the AndongCrowdfunding platform.

2.3 Supporters: Refers to Andong users who support the sponsor’s project . Supporters pre-purchase the derivative results and / or related products of the project by paying a certain amount of pre-purchase payment .

2.4 Fundraising success: refers to the amount of the contract concluded by the sponsor and the supporter with the project results and / or related products as the return target reaches or exceeds the fundraising set by the sponsor within the time set by the sponsor Amount.

2.5 Project success: After the fundraising of the project is successful, the initiator executes the project plan and finally forms the project outcome, and delivers the project results and / or related products to the supporter in accordance with the supporter’s pre-purchase order .

3.1 In order to protect the legitimate rights and interests of supporters, regulate the behavior of sponsors, and maintain the order of the AndongCrowdfunding platform, this agreement is specially formulated.

In the meantime , the legal consequences of using the AndongCrowdfunding platform shall be borne by the sponsors and supporters themselves.
